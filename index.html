NIR spectra

  • Light in 350-2500nm range
  • Quick to measure

How NIR is used

  • Primarily in empirical models
    • Multiple linear regression
    • Boosted regression trees
    • Partial Least Squares
    • Principal Component Regression

Problems

  • All narrow in their focus
  • No internal validation
    • If a prediction is off in obvious ways, it remains so

The solution?

Science!

  • Compare what we predict against what we know
    • If we get a low pH, we probably don't also have high calcium carbonate
  • Hence get models that mimic the scientific process
  • Should combine both empirical and mechanistic approaches

So what are you planning?

3 step process

  • Use Cubist and MCMC to generate empirical starting values
    • From NIR spectra from US projects
  • Use mechanistic models on empirical results
  • Predict carbon in Australia for the next 30 years
